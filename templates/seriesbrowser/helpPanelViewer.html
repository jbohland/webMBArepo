Scroll through the gallery using the arrows on the two sides of the filmstrip.


MouseOver the images to see the details in the info panel and to find out the relative position on the sagittal view.


Clicking on a thumbnail image will take display the actual image in the main panel.


For images with inherently high dynamic range, the dynamic range that you would like to see in the final 8 bit image can be set in the control panel on the top left by using the slider bars and then clicking on "Resample".
